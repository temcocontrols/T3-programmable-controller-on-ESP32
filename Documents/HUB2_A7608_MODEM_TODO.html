Integration Plan Block Diagram Firmware TODO Source Plan
Bring-up is in progress. Core LilyGo-compatible modem pins are confirmed, the HUB2 firmware AT debug bridge talks to the A7608E-H at fixed 115200 baud, and the ESP32-S3 USB-to-serial bridge is working. The latest HUB2 log entered the transparent AT bridge and returned +CPIN: READY after the UART TX/RX mapping was corrected. A later idle log showed escaped non-AT bytes after about 20 minutes; confirm whether the modem still answers AT after that event and check sleep/baud settings before treating it as a modem fault. The conservative AT status snapshot now verifies SIM ready, CSQ 14, LTE registration, packet attach, operator 46001, APN 3GNET, and IPv4 address 10.55.52.49. The reusable driver parser output is now validated against the HUB2 AT snapshot and reports CONNECTED with SIM, LTE registration, attach, operator, RSSI, and IPv4 status. The next focus is validating the newly added GNSS enable/read/disable path on hardware, then adding modem status fields and a small TCP/MQTT/HTTP upload test. Full 4G-to-Ethernet router behavior is intentionally outside this first task list.
